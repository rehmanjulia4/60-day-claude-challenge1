## What Was Built

An interactive **Customer & MVP Blueprint** for HealthNexus™, a proposed Executive Decision Intelligence Platform for healthcare organizations across the GCC and USA.

The deliverable reframes the Day 23 challenge prompt from a generic startup exercise into a substantive product strategy document grounded in 18+ years of real healthcare executive experience.

---

## Core Hypothesis

Healthcare organizations do not need another dashboard.

They need an **Executive Intelligence Layer** that connects data, governance, risk, performance, and recommended actions in one place.

Most organizations have:
- Data
- Dashboards
- AI projects

Very few have a governed framework that tells leaders:
- What changed
- Why it changed
- What risk it creates
- What action should be taken next

## MVP Recommendation

### Build First
- Executive Decision Intelligence Dashboard
- Governance Health Score (single composite score)
- KPI Integration Layer (clinical, financial, workforce, population health)
- Action Recommendations Engine
- Threshold-based alerts

### Do NOT Build in Phase 1
- Generative AI chatbot or assistant
- Predictive modeling marketplace
- Mobile application
- Patient-facing functionality
- Custom integrations for every customer
- Advanced benchmarking engine

---

## MoSCoW Prioritization

**Must Have:** Executive dashboard, governance score, KPI integration, action recommendations, alerts

**Should Have:** Trend analysis, benchmark comparisons, executive summaries

**Could Have:** AI-powered scenario planning, voice interface, board reporting automation

**Won't Have (Phase 1):** Patient portal, full EHR integration, chatbot, mobile app

## Key Learning

The biggest customer insight from Day 23:

Healthcare executives are not suffering from a lack of data. They are suffering from a lack of **decision intelligence**.

This insight fundamentally changed the MVP from an analytics platform to an Executive Intelligence Platform that answers four questions:

1. What changed?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What risk does it create?
4. What action should happen next?

## Evidence Base & Methodology Disclosure

Pain point rankings, buyer persona descriptions, pilot pricing tiers, and MVP success metric targets are founder hypotheses derived from 18+ years of healthcare executive experience across GCC and US markets. They are directional estimates, not survey-derived figures.

**Verified public data sources used in this blueprint:**

| Claim | Source | Year |
|---|---|---|
| 47% of healthcare data underutilized in decision-making | HIMSS & Arcadia Market Insights Survey, Data Analytics Platforms | 2024 |
| 68% of organizations have no immediate analytics/AI innovation strategy | HIMSS & Arcadia Market Insights Survey, Data Analytics Platforms | 2024 |
| 48% cite competing organizational priorities as top analytics barrier | HIMSS & Arcadia Market Insights Survey, Data Analytics Platforms | 2024 |
| 1-in-3 organizations report data less than 76% accurate | HIMSS & Arcadia Market Insights Pulse Survey | 2023 |
| Only 57% of org data used in key decision-making | HIMSS & Arcadia Market Insights Pulse Survey | 2023 |
| Clinicians spend ~28 hours/week on administrative tasks | Google Cloud & Harris Poll survey | 2024 |
| 80% of providers say admin burden takes time from patients | Google Cloud & Harris Poll, Chief Healthcare Executive | 2024 |
| Dashboard adoption shaped by social/organizational factors beyond technical design | Broughton et al., DOI: 10.1097/JMQ.0000000000000225 | 2025 |

All pilot success metric targets (usage rate, reporting reduction, actions per month) are hypotheses to be validated against per-site baselines during the pilot phase.
