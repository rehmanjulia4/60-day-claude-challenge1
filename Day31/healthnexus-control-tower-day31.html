How This Works

Your Role

You run supply chain operations for a live health system for a 3-minute shift. Alerts will appear describing real-world style disruptions: shortages, recalls, delays, failures and demand spikes.

KPIs

  • Patient Safety Index, Patient Satisfaction, Inventory Health and Logistics Efficiency rise with good decisions and fall with bad ones.
  • Operating Cost and Care Continuity Protected track the financial and clinical consequences of each choice.

Actions

Each alert offers several response options. Strong responses (expedite, activate backup, transfer) protect KPIs. Ignoring or delaying a critical alert lets it expire and damages your score.

Methodology Note

Alert categories are modeled on documented healthcare supply chain risk types (AHRMM, ASHP drug shortage reporting, FDA/MOHAP recall notices). Specific facility names, suppliers and figures are synthetic.
