## What I Built

**Executive Healthcare Investment Intelligence™ (EHII)** is a Claude-powered live analysis tool that evaluates publicly traded healthcare organizations through eight governance and performance dimensions designed by a healthcare executive with 18+ years across US and GCC health systems.

Unlike generic stock screeners, EHII integrates the same dimensions used in population health, AI governance, value-based care, and whole person care evaluation.

## Key Learning

The quality of an AI output is determined by the specificity of the lens you bring to it.

A generic investment analysis prompt returns generic investment analysis. A prompt authored by someone who has led analytics, AI governance, population health, and digital transformation across US and GCC health systems returns something genuinely different.

EHII is not a better stock screener. It is a different kind of question applied to the same data.

The same executive judgment used to evaluate whether a care plan aligns with a patient's values, whether a governance structure supports executive success, or whether a clinical AI tool produces measurable outcomes, also evaluates whether a healthcare organization is genuinely sustainable over the long term.

That is what eighteen years in this industry looks like when applied to investment intelligence.
