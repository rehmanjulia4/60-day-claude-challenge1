🎯 Personalized Recommendations — Abu Dhabi
🌬️
Indoor Air Improvements
Use HEPA air purifier — priority in bedroom and home office
Keep windows closed during shamal wind events (May–Sept)
Add indoor plants: peace lily, snake plant for natural filtration
Change AC filters every 4–6 weeks during summer months
🏃‍♀️
Outdoor Activity Guidance
Exercise outdoors before 8 AM — AQI typically 20–30% lower at dawn
Avoid outdoor cardio when AQI exceeds 100 (check IQAir or AirVisual app)
N95 mask recommended during dust storms — not standard surgical masks
Hydrate aggressively — heat amplifies particulate respiratory impact
💇‍♀️
Hair & Scalp Care (Hard Water)
Install shower filter — removes chlorine, calcium, and high-TDS minerals
Use chelating shampoo weekly to remove mineral buildup from hair shaft
Deep condition with oil-based mask every 7–10 days
Final rinse with filtered or bottled water significantly reduces breakage
🧴
Skin Care — Arid + Hard Water
Use oil-based cleanser — preserves natural lipid barrier stripped by hard water
Apply ceramide-based moisturizer immediately post-shower while skin is damp
SPF 50 daily — UAE UV index reaches 11–12 (extreme) April–October
Humidifier in bedroom — target 40–50% relative humidity indoors
💧
Water Quality Improvements
Install reverse osmosis under-sink filter for drinking water
Avoid boiling tap water — concentrates TDS and mineral load
Test home water TDS — Abu Dhabi tap typically 300–600 ppm (ideal is under 150)
Consider alkaline mineral water brands certified for UAE market
