# Day 5: Context Engineering
## ABTalks 60-Day Claude AI Challenge

---

## Objective

Understand how context engineering improves AI outputs by providing relevant background information, goals, constraints, and personal details.

---

## Prompt A (Without Context)

### Prompt

```
Create a 30-day learning roadmap.

Include:
- Weekly milestones
- Daily tasks
- Resources
- Projects
- Final outcome

Make it practical and beginner-friendly.
```

### Output Summary

The roadmap was generic and assumed a complete beginner. It lacked personalization and did not account for professional experience, industry knowledge, available time, or career goals. The output could have been given to anyone in any field.

**What Claude produced:**

- Week 1: Learn AI basics
- Week 2: Learn prompting
- Week 3: Build projects
- Week 4: Create a portfolio



---

## Prompt B (With Executive Healthcare Context)

### Prompt

```
Create a 30-day learning roadmap.

Context:

Current Situation:
Senior Healthcare Data and Analytics Executive with 17+ years of experience across the
United States, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and India. Former Section Director of Analytics &
Reporting for the Department of Health Abu Dhabi and former Director of Healthcare Data
Science & Analytics for the Ministry of National Guard Health Affairs in Saudi Arabia.

Current Skills:
Healthcare Analytics, Health Informatics, Data Governance, Population Health, AI Strategy,
Digital Transformation, Healthcare Operations, Executive Leadership, Power BI, Tableau,
SQL, Healthcare Performance Management, Value Based Care, Healthcare Policy

Goal:
Become one of the leading healthcare AI and data transformation executives in the Middle
East while building a personal brand as a thought leader in AI, digital health, healthcare
analytics, and healthcare transformation.

Available Time:
1 hour per day

Experience Level:
Intermediate AI user with advanced healthcare leadership experience

Preferred Learning Style:
Projects, executive case studies, healthcare use cases, and practical implementation

Include:
- Weekly milestones
- Daily tasks
- Resources
- Projects
- Final outcome

Make it practical and beginner-friendly.
```

### Output Summary

The roadmap was tailored specifically for a healthcare executive seeking to become a leader in AI-driven healthcare transformation. The recommendations aligned with healthcare analytics, AI governance, digital health, and executive leadership goals. Every week had a clear executive purpose.

**What Claude produced:**

- Week 1: Healthcare AI Foundations — Generative AI in healthcare, FDA and GCC AI regulations, HIMSS AI maturity models, healthcare LLM use cases
- Week 2: Executive AI Leadership — AI governance, responsible AI, data strategy, AI operating models
- Week 3: Healthcare AI Transformation — Build an AI governance framework, AI value realization model, healthcare AI maturity assessment
- Week 4: Thought Leadership — Create a LinkedIn content series, conference presentation, healthcare AI whitepaper, executive AI roadmap

## Comparison

| | Prompt A | Prompt B |
|---|---|---|
| Which felt more personalized? | No | **Yes** |
| Which would I actually follow? | No | **Yes** |
| Tailored to my industry? | No | **Yes** |
| Aligned to my career goals? | No | **Yes** |
| Actionable for my level? | No | **Yes** |

**Why Prompt B was better:**

Prompt B incorporated:
- Professional experience (17+ years, multiple countries)
- Industry expertise (healthcare analytics, AI strategy)
- Learning preferences (case studies, executive use cases)
- Available time (1 hour per day)
- Career goals (leading healthcare AI executive in the Middle East)

The recommendations were significantly more relevant and immediately actionable.

---

## Key Learning

The quality of AI outputs depends heavily on the quality of context provided.

**Prompting tells AI what to do.**
**Context tells AI who you are, what you know, and what success looks like.**

The addition of context transformed a generic learning plan into an executive-level healthcare AI development roadmap.

---

## Real-World Healthcare Application

As a healthcare executive, context engineering can improve:

- Strategic planning and AI roadmap design
- AI governance framework development
- Population health analytics programs
- Executive decision support tools
- Healthcare transformation programs
- Board-level presentations and proposals
- Digital health roadmaps for health systems

Organizations that effectively provide context to AI systems will achieve significantly better outcomes than those relying solely on generic prompts.
